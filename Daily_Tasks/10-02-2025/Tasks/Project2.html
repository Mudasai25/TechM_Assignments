Tribute to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel (1875-1950) was a key leader in India's independence movement and the first Deputy Prime Minister and Home Minister of India. He played a crucial role in the integration of over 560 princely states into the Indian Union, earning him the title 'Iron Man of India'.

His leadership, vision, and commitment to national unity continue to inspire generations. His efforts laid the foundation for a strong and united India.
